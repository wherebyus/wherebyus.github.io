The New Tropicana at the Wolfsonian

We worked with the Wolfsonian-FIU to design The New Tropicana, a celebration of Cuban history and culture that would bring out hundreds of locals to experience their new exhibit, “Promising Paradise.” Through a creative campaign in our daily and weekly newsletters, we helped bring out more than 500 Miamians to eat, drink, dance, and discover together.

  • Launched: May 2016
  • Check out: The New Tropicana at the Wolfsonian

Florida Grand Opera

Understanding Miami’s millennials

Florida Grand Opera has an international reputation for groundbreaking work—and for approaching the arts with an eye toward innovation and experimentation. They’ve taken opera beyond the hall with their Unexpected Opera in Unexpected Places series; explored new formats; and focused on young artists. We helped FGO understand how to engage the millennial demographic with an in-depth research project that identified new opportunities for marketing and audience development.

Road Trip with Chevrolet

Tapping wanderlust to reach Miami’s adventurers

Because we’re a tourism city, few brands spend time speaking to Miami’s own explorers and travelers. We took Miamians on a journey to the Florida Keys, powered by Chevrolet’s Trax SUV. The trip included a video journey with local social influencer Woo Supreme, an interactive timeline of local stops in the Keys, and a photo essay of people and places we found, all designed to engage Miami’s adventurers.

  • Launched: September 2015
  • Check out: Road Trip with Chevrolet

El Tamalero

Celebrating Miami’s unique characters

Romelio Reyes has been selling his legendary tamales in Hialeah for decades. The 85-year-old is something of a local celebrity: his tamales are said to cure arthritis and other maladies, and anyone who spends time in Hialeah has seen him on his bike. We caught up with him to share his story. The video has been viewed more than 50,000 times, as part of a neighborhood guide for Lyft that reached more than 250,000 locals.

  • Launched: July 2016
  • Check out: El Tamalero video

Idea Center

Building participation with Miami’s curious locals

Miami-Dade College’s Idea Center is an innovative hub for entrepreneurship, bringing students and community members together for educational opportunities and experiential learning on technology, human-centered design, and entrepreneurship. We helped the Idea Center find new participants for its continuing education courses through creative campaigns in our newsletter.
